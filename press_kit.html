Press Kit

Abduct Media is an XR / VR / AR development studio based in Nürnberg, Germany, founded in April 2024 by Italian brothers Davide and Giovanni Cocchiara.

We are currently producing the multi-user XR investigative experience "The Demise of the ISS Leonardo".

We are about to start production of the multimedia documentary "Library of Dreams".

We also deal with a variety of multimedia content: books, games, comics.
